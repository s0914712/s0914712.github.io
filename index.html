Taiwan Defense & China Military Intelligence

臺灣防衛實力 & 中國軍事動態研究

This platform is dedicated to strengthening awareness of Taiwan's defense posture and providing data-driven insights into China's military activities. Through open-source intelligence (OSINT), machine learning, and satellite data analysis, we aim to make complex military dynamics accessible and transparent.

本平台致力於強化對臺灣防衛態勢的認識，並透過資料驅動的方式洞察中國軍事活動。結合開源情報（OSINT）、機器學習與衛星數據分析，讓複雜的軍事動態變得透明且易於理解。

Taiwan Gray Zone Monitor

臺灣灰色地帶監測

A monitoring system based on CSIS methodology that tracks suspicious vessels in Taiwan's surrounding waters. Integrates SAR satellite imagery and AIS data to detect dark ships and military exercise zone activities.

基於 CSIS 方法論的臺灣周邊海域可疑船隻監測系統，整合 SAR 衛星影像與 AIS 數據，追蹤暗船與軍演區活動。

PLA Data Dashboard

解放軍軍事數據儀表板

An interactive dashboard analyzing PLA aircraft sorties and military activities around Taiwan. Features real-time data visualization, machine learning predictions, and comprehensive Taiwan Strait security monitoring.

互動式儀表板，分析解放軍在臺灣周邊的軍機架次與軍事活動，提供即時數據視覺化、機器學習預測及全面的臺海安全監測。
